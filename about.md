Born in Pelotas, Brazil, Davi Raubach Tuchtenhagen (1992) began studying composition in 2012 at the Federal University of Pelotas (Bachelor of Music). He concluded his master program in 2019 under the guidance of Felipe de Almeida Ribeiro at the Federal University of Paraná. In this institution, integrated the new music research group Núcleo Música Nova. He is simultaneously engaged in composition, accordion, songwriting and teaching activities.
