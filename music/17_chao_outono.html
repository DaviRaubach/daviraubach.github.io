Chão de outono

Published: Maio 27, 2025

for flute and electronics

2017-8

Premiered by Valentina Daldegan. "Chão de outono" is the result of a collaboration between the composer and the flutist Valentina Daldegan. It is based on the homonymous poem of Mario Quintana (Chão de outono). This is a possible translation for it:'

Autumn Floor

Throughout the uneven stones of the paving, poor yellow leaves in a panic pass by, pursued by an invitation of burial, sinister, crackling, each time nearer and nearer, two wings dressed in black!
